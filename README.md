# Jordan Bankston – Computer Science ePortfolio

## Welcome
This ePortfolio showcases my growth throughout the Computer Science program at Southern New Hampshire University. It highlights the work I have completed, the improvements I made to that work, and how my overall approach to software development has evolved.

When I first started, my focus was mainly on getting code to work. Now, I think more about design, efficiency, security, and how real users interact with what I build. This portfolio reflects that shift.

My long-term goal is to pursue a career in cybersecurity, with the intention of becoming an Air Force officer or potentially joining the Space Force. I am especially interested in protecting systems, organizations, and individuals from cyber threats.
